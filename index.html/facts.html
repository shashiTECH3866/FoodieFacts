🍎 Fun Food Facts!

  • 🍞 The smell of bread baking can make people kinder — it boosts good mood instantly!
  • 🍝 Spaghetti wasn’t invented in Italy — ancient noodles were found in China over 4,000 years ago!
  • 🧂 Salt was once so valuable, it was used as currency (that's where the word "salary" comes from)!
  • 🧄 Garlic can glow in the dark when cooked with acids — totally safe and naturally magical!
  • 🍫 Chocolate was once a drink only for royalty — the Aztecs called it “food of the gods.”
  • 🍳 Eggs age faster at room temperature than in the fridge — they lose moisture and float when old!
  • 🍚 Rice is eaten by more than half the world every single day — truly a global favorite!
  • 🍯 Honey never spoils — archaeologists found 3,000-year-old edible honey in Egyptian tombs!
  • 🧀 There are over 2,000 types of cheese in the world — one for every mood!
  • 🥑 Avocados are berries, but strawberries aren't true berries! Weird, right?🤯
  • 🥕 Carrots were originally purple — the orange variety was created in the Netherlands!
  • 🥔 The potato was the first vegetable grown in space by NASA!🚀
  • 🍕 The world's most expensive pizza costs over $10,000 and includes gold and caviar!
  • 🌮 Tacos are older than you think — they’ve been around since before the Spanish arrived in Mexico.
  • 🧁Cupcakes used to be baked in teacups — that’s where the name comes from!
