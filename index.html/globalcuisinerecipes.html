Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 12 ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix mascarpone cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  2. Dip each ladyfinger briefly in coffee and layer in a dish.
  3. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese mixture on top.
  5. Dust with cocoa powder.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
